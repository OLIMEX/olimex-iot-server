The service is located at https://iot.olimex.com

To use our free OlimexIoT service you should

  1. Own some of our IoT products
  2. Sign up for the service
  3. Setup your IoT node

Sign up for OlimexIoT Service

Open your favorite browser and go to https://iot.olimex.com/signup

Fill the form and click Sing Up button.

An activation message will be send to your e-mail address and you will be redirected to the activation page.

After receiving the e-mail message you can copy and paste the activation code or click on activation link.

You are now ready to use our service.
